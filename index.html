Welcome to My Personal Website

I’m Besir Zeneli, a biologist, educator, and athletics veteran from North Macedonia with a deep passion for science, youth development, and making meaningful connections across cultures. My journey began at Hacettepe University, where I earned my degree in biology, and since then, I’ve dedicated myself to helping students discover the wonders of the natural world. As a biology teacher at Marif International School, I’m not just teaching facts—I’m guiding the next generation of thinkers and innovators, helping them understand the impact they can have on the world around them. Outside the classroom, my heart has always been in athletics. As a former national team runner and veteran of the sport, I know firsthand the power of discipline, perseverance, and teamwork. My involvement in North Macedonia's athletic community goes beyond just competing; it’s about inspiring others and contributing to a legacy of growth and excellence in our sport.
